About Barasoc

Barasoc is an idea floated at the heart of Australian Capital Territory by the students of Canberra Institute of Technology as their final year project back in 2019. It started its business by working for different individuals, businesses and educational organizations by providing consulting and educational services. Barasoc's non-profit goal is to give skills to the poor community of the world in the field of cybersecurity.

Our Services

  • Pen Testing
  • SOC Training
  • Planning and Development
  • Incident Response Training
  • Consultation
