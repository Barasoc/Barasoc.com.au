Welcome to Barasoc

Barasoc is a cyber security company based in Canberra, Australia, specializing in building incident response plans and Security Operation Centers. We offer a range of services, including pentesting, threat assessment, cyber security training, and research & development.

Our company head office is located in Canberra, Australia, and we are currently working with the Canberra Institute of Technology and the University of New South Wales on different community training programs. Barasoc has helped CIT and UNSW in building lab manuals related to threat assessment, incident response, and SOC projects.
